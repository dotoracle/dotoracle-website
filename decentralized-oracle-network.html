Decentralized oracle network

DotOracle network is a chain-less decentralized network. To achieve fast confirmation of the network, we propose to use the following techniques.

  • Fast, Secure, decentralized network

  • Practical Byzantine Fault Tolerance (PBFT) distributed consensus protocol

  • Optimization with the Elliptic Curve (EC) Multisignature scheme

  • Transperancy, MoonBeam Polkadot integration

Our Mission

Our mission is to create a trusted world, where every off-chain information can be reliably pushed to the Polkadot ecosystem.

By having off-chain information updated to Polkadot in real-time, on-chain application can utilize the oraclized data to provide trusted, censorship-resistem service to all people in the world.

Our Vision

Web3 is the ultimate destination of all blockchain ecosystems. We don't believe a single blockchain can be sufficient to serve all blockchain applications.


The ultimate world of blockchain technology should be the interoperation of all blockchains and all off-chain applications. To implement this vision, a middleware layer like DotOracle providing real-time on-chain and off-chain connection is not just a compliment but a must.
